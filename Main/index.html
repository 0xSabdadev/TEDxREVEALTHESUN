What is Reveal The Sun?

The theme we want to purpose is REVEAL THE SUN. Inspired by the power of the sun, the world's main energy source. The sun always radiates its power, even at night, the sun still transfers its energy by reflecting to the moon, then lighting the earth.

The Idea of REVEAL THE SUN. is to let the audience know how actually the sun is: like the phenomenon, the materials inside, the secret why the sun is the power source to the world and revealing the sun from the science point of view. Luckily, the idea of REVEAL THE SUN. and the discussion according to the sun phenomenon can be linked to the Climate Change matters, and how actually the sun power works and how to use the power of the sun to solve this matter, since this matter is not experienced by only several people but also has a wide impact to the global. Besides these ideas, we want to bring the idea of someone who is becoming the sun for his society, not only having the power yet willing to do more action and gives so many impacts to enlighten the local society and communities.

By the theme of REVEAL THE SUN, we want to bring new ideas to the local community here in Semarang. This theme is diverse to three main ideas: Apollo, Aurora, and Twilight.

  • What is REVEAL THE SUN?

    Started with TEDxUndip in 2019 which affiliated with BEM FEB Undip, now TEDxUNDIP 2.0 is back with a fully independent background. TEDxUNDIP 2.0 designed to help our communities to spark conversation and connection through TED experiences at the university level. Local, National, and Global ideas come to the center of Java to share ideas worth spreading.

  • What is the relevance of the theme?

    Apollo is also believed as the most insightful God among the other Gods. We depict Apollo as the God who knows the component of the sun, the phenomenon that occurs because of the sun based on the research they've collected, and also the activists who know how to use the energy of the sun not only for temporal occasion, but on their daily life and capable to influence their society.

    Aurora is a natural light display in the Earth's sky as the result of disturbances in the magnetosphere caused by the solar wind. Aurora is mostly known as a beautiful solar phenomenon yet caused by the disturbance, then the beautiful light with multiple colors appears and radiates into the earth. We elaborate this idea to someone who lives with the disturbance but not being disturbed, even make a great thing by utilizing the disturbance itself. We would like to radiate this idea for the local society to dare to make a different action no matter how big the disturbance, the hassle, yet dare to utilize the disturbance into an inspiring movement.

    Twilight on Earth is the illumination of the lower atmosphere when the Sun itself is not directly visible because it is below the horizon. When the Sun reaches 18° below the horizon, the twilight's brightness is nearly zero, and evening twilight becomes night time. When the Sun again reaches 18° below the horizon, nighttime becomes morning twilight. We would like to depict this idea to be someone who is in the “twilight” age -- the age of about to rise (young age) and the age of about to dawn (old age) but willing to empower society to do more action.
